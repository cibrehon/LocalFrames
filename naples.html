The Spanish Quarter - QuartIeri Spagnoli - is the most densely populated neighborhood in the city of Naples, Italy.

Built on a hillside where old and narrow streets mingle in the shadows of neglected buildings that stand against time, where laundry racks stand on the cobblestones and motorcycles echo in the streets; this neighborhood's 14,000 residents are a tight-knit religious community where young and old live together.

QuartIeri Spagnoli is part of my continuing interest in photographing the poverty-stricken cities of the world, where religion, hope, and orderly chaos have taken together a large part of society's daily life.
