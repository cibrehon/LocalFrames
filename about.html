EGE ILICAK

Artist Bio

Ege Ilicak (b. 1988, Turkey) is a documentary and street photographer based in Heidelberg, Germany and Ankara, Turkey.

Ege developed his passion for photography after completing his education in informatics at Bilkent University and chose it as a secondary profession besides his engineering work. In 2021, he received master classes from Cristina de Middel and Yael Martinez at Magnum Photos in Paris.

Ege's work captures people's daily lives in their urban environments and explores various forms of expressions at the crossroads of documentary and street photography.
